Kinochain Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Data collection

Kinochain does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. The app has no accounts or login and does not track its users.

Network requests

The app makes read-only requests to The Movie Database (TMDB) API to retrieve public movie and person information. These requests do not include personal data.

Analytics and advertising

Kinochain does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services.

Data stored on your device

The app stores a small onboarding preference on your device. It never leaves your device.

Third-party service

Movie data is provided by TMDB. You can read the TMDB privacy policy.

Contact

For privacy questions, email info@rightword.be.
